Click ▶ Record test to start a 10-second timed run at the current speed. WAITING events during the run are recorded.

How to read this: WAITING fires when the player advances faster than the decoder can deliver frames. With everything in memory, WAITING == decoder/CPU bottleneck. Compare videos with low vs high WAITING counts at the same speed; look at codec, resolution × fps × rate, avg GOP, and bitrate. Long GOP (>60 frames) means high-speed playback walks long P-frame chains and gets expensive. H.265/AV1 without hardware decode is much slower than H.264.
